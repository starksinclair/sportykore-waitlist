2. How Do We Process Your Information?

In Short: We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our Services, communicate with you, for security and fraud prevention, and to comply with law.

We process your personal information for the following reasons:

  • To authenticate users: We use email addresses to send one-time passwords (OTP) that verify user identity and grant secure access to the platform.
  • To deliver and facilitate the delivery of services: Core app functionality including competitions, games, stats, and standings.
  • To manage competition participation: We use player profile information to facilitate roster management, match participation, and performance tracking within competitions.
  • To calculate and display sports statistics: We use match data and player identifiers to automatically calculate goals, assists, appearances, standings, and other sports statistics.
  • To send administrative information: OTP codes, welcome emails, Terms update notifications, and competition invite notifications.
  • To respond to user inquiries: Support communications via our contact email.
  • To enforce rate limiting and prevent abuse: We monitor API request patterns and IP addresses to detect and prevent excessive or abusive use of the platform.
  • To maintain platform integrity: We log match data entry patterns to detect suspicious activity such as falsified scores or statistics.
  • To investigate and resolve disputes: We retain logs of match data changes, invite acceptances, and account activity to assist in resolving disputes between competition owners, team owners, and players.
  • To identify usage trends: To analyze how our Services are used so we can improve them.
  • To protect our Services: To diagnose problems and prevent fraudulent activities.

7. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

In Short: We keep your information for as long as you have an account with us.

We retain your personal information for as long as you maintain an account with Sportykore. Upon account deletion:

  • Personal account data (email address, display name, bio, and profile photo) will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Match statistics, game records, and competition history associated with your player profile will be retained indefinitely as part of the competition's historical record but will be anonymized and no longer linked to your identity
  • Authentication codes (OTPs) are automatically deleted within 10 minutes of generation regardless of whether they are used
  • Device tokens are deleted immediately upon account deletion

When we have no ongoing legitimate business need to process your personal information, we will either delete or anonymize such information.

15. Sports Statistics and Competition Records

Match statistics, game results, player performance data, and competition standings entered into Sportykore by competition administrators and team owners constitute competition records. These records are retained indefinitely to preserve the integrity and history of competitions.

Upon account deletion, your personal identifiers (name, email, avatar) will be removed and your player record anonymized, but associated match statistics and competition history will be retained as part of the competition's permanent record.

Competition owners are responsible for the accuracy of data entered on behalf of their competitions.

16. Invite Links and Player Roster Data

Competition administrators may generate invite links to add users to competitions. When you accept an invite link, your player profile information including your display name and avatar will be visible to:

  • All members and administrators of that competition
  • Any user who views that competition's public page
  • General users browsing the Sportykore platform

Competition administrators may view, edit, and remove player roster entries within their competitions. If you wish to be removed from a competition roster, contact your competition administrator directly or contact us at legal@notifications.sportykore.com.

17. One-Time Password (OTP) Authentication

Sportykore uses a passwordless authentication system. When you sign in, a one-time password (OTP) is sent to your email address via our email provider Resend. This code is:

  • Valid for 10 minutes only
  • Single-use — can only be used once
  • Permanently deleted immediately after use or upon expiry
  • Stored in encrypted form during its validity window

Sportykore does not store passwords of any kind. Your email address is the sole authentication credential associated with your account.

Lost email access? If you lose access to your email address, contact us at legal@notifications.sportykore.com to initiate an account recovery process.
