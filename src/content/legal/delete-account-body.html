This page explains how to request deletion of a Sportykore account, the mobile app published by Sinclair Nzenwata, doing business as Sportykore, and exactly what happens to your data afterward. You do not need to open the app or sign in to make this request.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to info@sportykore.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Send it from the email address registered to your Sportykore account. This is how we confirm the request is really from you, requests from a different address may need extra verification.
  4. Include the full name on your account

We will confirm your request by reply email. Once verified, deletion is completed within 30 days.

Prefer to copy the address instead of tapping it? info@sportykore.com

What is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following personal data is permanently removed:

  • Email address and login details
  • Profile photo
  • Date of birth
  • Bio, preferred foot, height, and position details
  • City, state, and nationality
  • Social media handle
  • Highlight video links
  • The connection between your account and any player record

What is kept, and why

Goals, appearances, and other match events recorded during games stay part of the match and league history. These records are anonymized — they are no longer linked to you, your name, or any personal details. They appear in the record as a former player.

We keep them because match results and league tables are shared records that other teams and players rely on. Removing them entirely would change completed match results and league standings for everyone else in that competition.

No personal data remains in these anonymized records.

Competitions and teams you created are not deleted, because they belong to the competition and its other members, not to one account. Ownership of a competition can be transferred to someone else by contacting info@sportykore.com from the account's registered email address.

Retention period

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days of a verified request.
  • Backup copies are fully purged within 90 days.
  • Anonymized match records are kept indefinitely as part of competition history, and contain no personal information.

Questions

For any question about this process, or about your data, contact info@sportykore.com.
