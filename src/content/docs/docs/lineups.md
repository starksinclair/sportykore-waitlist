This page helps organizers and team admins submit official lineups for a match.

## Before you start

- You must be the league organizer or an active team admin for one of the game's teams.
- The game must not be full-time or cancelled.
- The team needs enough active roster players.

## Steps

1. Open the team lineup hub from **Manage** or the team admin route.
2. Choose the match.
3. Pick a formation.
4. Fill every formation slot.
5. Add exactly 11 starters.
6. Add up to 12 substitutes.
7. Save the lineup.

## Rules & good to know

- Lineups must have exactly 11 starters.
- Every formation slot must be filled.
- A lineup can have up to 12 substitutes.
- Players must be unique across starters and substitutes.
- Lineups lock when a game is full-time or cancelled.
- Team admins are scoped to their assigned team.
- Formation endpoints are public.
- Formation records include slots and positions used by lineup validation.
- Substitution events recorded later do not mutate the submitted starting lineup view.
