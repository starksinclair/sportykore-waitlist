This page lists important current limits so organizers do not plan around features that are not usable yet.

## Available now

- Public browsing for leagues, matches, teams, players, standings, stats, brackets, venues, and public lineups.
- Email code signup, sign-in, recovery, logout, and account deletion.
- League/round-robin competitions.
- Knockout competitions and knockout seasons.
- Seasons with inactive, active, and completed statuses.
- Team management, invite links/codes, roster editing, and team admins.
- Saved venues and one-off fixture venue names.
- Fixture scheduling, match clock controls, score updates, goal accreditation, cards, saves, substitutions, penalties, and full-time completion.
- Lineup builder with formations, starters, and substitutes.
- Automatic round-robin standings and tiebreakers.
- Player profiles and career stats.
- Country browsing, global search, favourites, cached read browsing, and live game updates.

## Coming soon or not yet available

These items were marked schema-ready, not implemented, not found, or not clearly usable in the reference. They are listed only so you know not to rely on them yet.

- Group stages.
- Playoff stages as a distinct format.
- Stage groups and grouped standings.
- Stored/manual `current_minute` as a user-facing feature.
- Lineup subbed-in/subbed-out minute fields that update the live pitch view.
- Offline write queue for creating, scoring, stats, or roster edits.
- Point deductions.
- Manual standings adjustments.
- Promotion, relegation, and playoff-zone highlighting.
- Dedicated in-play penalty stat type.
- Individual shootout takers and scored/missed attempts.
- Mobile controls for shots on target and fouls conceded.
- Adding a cup/knockout stage to an existing round-robin season from a clearly supported mobile path.
- Editing recovery email after signup.
