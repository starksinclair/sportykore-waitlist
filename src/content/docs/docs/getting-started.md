This page helps you understand Sportykore's main roles before you create, manage, or join a competition.

## Before you start

- You can browse public competitions without signing in.
- You need to sign in before you create, manage, favourite, join, or edit anything.

## Steps

1. Open Sportykore and complete onboarding.
2. Browse the home feed, search, or country pages to see public competitions.
3. Sign in with an email code when the app asks you to.
4. If you are an organizer, open **Create** or **Manage**.
5. If you are a player, open an invite link or enter an invite code from your organizer.

## Roles

### Public viewer

Public viewers can open public league, match, team, player, country, standings, stats, bracket, venue, and lineup pages without signing in.

### League organizer

The organizer owns a competition. Organizers can create and edit leagues, seasons, teams, venues, fixtures, stages, scores, stats, invites, and roster rows.

### Team admin

A team admin is assigned by the organizer. Team admins can manage lineups and open match-day tools for their own assigned team. They do not get full league management access.

### Player

Players join through invite links or codes. When a player accepts an invite, Sportykore creates or connects their player profile so their team, season, match, and career stats can appear publicly.

## Rules & good to know

- Public read routes do not require sign-in.
- Favouriting, creating, managing, joining, and lineup editing require sign-in.
- Organizer-only actions are checked against league ownership.
- Team admins are scoped to their assigned team.
- Player profile creation currently happens through invite acceptance.
