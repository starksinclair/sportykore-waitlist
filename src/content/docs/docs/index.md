Use these guides to create a competition, add teams and players, run fixtures, and keep public scores, tables, brackets, and player profiles up to date.

Sportykore is built for grassroots football organizers and players. A competition has seasons, teams, players, fixtures, venues, standings, and stages. A stage can be round-robin or knockout.

## Before you start

- Use the mobile app.
- Sign in with an email code when you need to create, manage, favourite, join, or edit anything.
- Public pages can be viewed without signing in.

## Start here

1. Read [Getting started](/docs/getting-started/) to understand roles.
2. Use [Sign in with email code](/docs/sign-in/) to get into your account.
3. Follow [Create your first competition](/docs/first-competition/) to set up a league or knockout.
4. Add teams and players with [Teams, rosters, and invites](/docs/teams-rosters-invites/).
5. Run games from [Fixtures and live match day](/docs/fixtures-match-day/).

## Good to know

- Public viewers can browse competitions, matches, teams, players, standings, brackets, venues, and public lineups.
- Organizers own competitions and manage settings, seasons, teams, venues, games, stages, score updates, stats, invites, and roster changes.
- Team admins can manage lineups and match day for their assigned team.
- Players join through invite links or codes and get public profiles with career stats.
- Recently loaded screens can stay visible with limited connection, but creating, scoring, and editing still require a connection.
