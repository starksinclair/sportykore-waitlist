This page helps organizers build teams and rosters for a season.

## Before you start

- You must be the competition organizer to add teams, generate invites, edit roster rows, or assign team admins.
- Players need an invite link or code to join a roster.

## Add, edit, or delete teams

1. Open **Manage**.
2. Select your competition.
3. Open **Teams**.
4. Tap **Add team**, or open an existing team to edit.
5. Add or update the team name and optional logo.
6. Save.

## Assign team admins

1. Open **Manage > Teams**.
2. Open a team edit sheet.
3. Search users by name or email.
4. Assign or remove team admins.
5. Save.

## Invite players to a team

1. Open **Manage > Players**.
2. Tap **Invite to team**.
3. Select the team.
4. Generate the invite link.
5. Share the link or code with the player.

## Player joins with an invite

1. Open the invite link, or paste the code in **Join League**.
2. Sign in or create an account.
3. If asked, complete player profile creation.
4. Join the team roster.
5. Open your player profile from **Profile** or public pages.

<!-- SCREENSHOT: invite link sheet and Join League screen -->

## Edit roster details

1. Open **Manage > Players**.
2. Pick a team.
3. Tap a player row.
4. Edit jersey number, position, or captain flag.
5. Save, or remove the player from the roster if needed.

## Rules & good to know

- Teams belong to the whole league.
- Deleting a team cascades through related games, standings, roster entries, stats, and invites.
- At least two teams are needed to schedule games.
- Invites expire after 7 days.
- Invites are copied or shared by the organizer. The backend does not email player invites automatically.
- If a user has no player profile, Sportykore asks them to create one during invite acceptance.
- Roster statuses include active, transferred, injured, suspended, pending, and inactive.
- The observed mobile edit sheet exposes jersey number, position, and captain flag. It displays status but does not edit it.
- Positions are attack, midfield, defence, and goalkeeper.
- Team admins can manage lineups and match day for their assigned team only.
